Our Story

We were founded by like-minded pet lovers who have a deep appreciation for commonly overlooked pets, the rock. We recognize that your pet rock is just as important and requires the same amount of care as other traditional house pets. We began our journey with one goal in mind: We want to create a safe and nuturing environments for your pet rocks for when you need some help.
